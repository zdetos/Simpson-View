<b> Load FID/SPE </b>
<br> Load SIMPSON spectrum or FID using File -> Load FID/SPE menu
<br><b> Zoom </b>
<br> Draw rectangle with mouse left click
<br><b> Pan </b>
<br> Drag mouse with right click
<br><b> Scale y axis </b>
<br> Mouse wheel up or down
<br><b> Scale x axis </b>
<br> Hold Shift and mouse wheel up or down
<br><b> Reset </b>
<br> right double click
<br><b> Hide/Show line </b>
<br> Left click on line in legend
<br><b> Select line </b>
<br> Left click on text in legend
<br><b> Delete line </b>
<br> Right click on text in legend or Chart -> Clear selected line
<br><b> Crosshair </b>
  <br> - Activated by Chart checkbox 
  <br> - Crosshair sticks to selected line
  <br> - Left double click starts/cancels distance measurement
<br><b> Reset Chart </b>
<br> Using menu Chart -> Clear all lines
<br><b> Line context menu </b>
<br> Right click on text in legend
 <br> - Delete line
 <br> - Set scalling factor
 <br> - Toggle real/imaginary
